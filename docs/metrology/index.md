# Metrology

The metrology subdomain covers measurement, inspection, verification, and the supporting environment required to produce trustworthy observations.

## Scope

- dimensional metrology
- visual and optical inspection
- inspection lighting
- surface, edge, burr, marking, and solder-joint inspection
- calibration and verification practices
- measurement fixtures
- environmental control for inspection and measurement
- data capture and SPC interfaces
- instrument storage and protection

## Doctrine

Metrology capability is not defined only by owning instruments. A measurement or inspection method becomes dependable when the instrument, fixture, lighting, environment, procedure, and verification path work together.
